HUESTON WOODS STATE PARK

In the spring of 2017, volunteers constructed and installed 38 bluebird nesting boxes at Hueston Woods State Park Golf Course in Oxford, Ohio. Since then, volunteers have checked the houses on a weekly basis and recorded the activities in each house, such as number of eggs, dates of hatching, indications of predation, and when nestlings fledged. The plan is for this project to last for 10 years.
